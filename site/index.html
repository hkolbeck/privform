Welcome to PrivForm.net

We aim to provide a way for people to collect sensitive data as securely as possible.

The service is currently in a closed alpha.

PrivForm.net uses a technology called asymmetric encryption (PGP to be exact) to ensure that the responses to your forms are only unencrypted when your respondents enter them and when you read them. When you're setting up a form you'll generate a keypair, a private key and a public key. You'll save the private key file to your computer and send us the public key. When people respond, their responses will be encrypted with the public key before they're sent over the internet to us, and only the private key that's never been seen by anyone but you can unencrypt them. For details, please check out the README on Github, linked in lower right corner.
